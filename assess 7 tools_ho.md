###### LinkedIn Data Extraction Workflow using Scrupp

###### Scenario



Use Case: Finding candidates for a Software Developer role

Target Profiles: Junior and Mid-level Software Developers on LinkedIn



###### Data Fields to Extract

**Full Name**

Identifies the candidate for communication

**Job Title**

Helps check if the candidate matches the required role

**Company Name**

Shows current workplace and experience level

**Location**

Ensures the candidate is in the preferred region

**Email (if available)**

Used for direct outreach outside LinkedIn



###### Workflow Process

1\. Search Setup

* Use LinkedIn search with filters:
* Keywords: “Software Developer”, “Backend Developer”
* Experience Level: Entry to Mid-level
* Location: India (or specific city)
* 

2\. Data Extraction with Scrupp

* Scrupp automatically scans selected LinkedIn profiles
* Extracts key details (name, title, company, location, email)
* Saves the data into a structured format (Google Sheets/CSV)



3\. Qualification Step

* Filter extracted data based on:
* Job Title contains: “Software Developer” or “Engineer”
* Location: Must be in target city/region
* Experience level based on company or role
* Only matching profiles are kept in the final list



4\. Final Output

* Clean and organized list of candidates
* Ready for:

&#x09;HR review

&#x09;Interview shortlisting



###### Outreach via email or LinkedIn

* How This Replaces Manual Work
* No need to open profiles one by one
* No manual copy-paste into spreadsheets
* Faster data collection in bulk
* Reduces human error and missing information
* Saves hours of repetitive work



###### Outcome

Efficient candidate sourcing process

Structured and ready-to-use candidate database

More time for actual hiring decisions instead of data collection
