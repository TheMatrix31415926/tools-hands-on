### Lead Search and Qualification Workflow with Apollo

## Use Case

**Search Goal:** Finding potential B2B clients  
**Target Audience:** Founders, CEOs, and Marketing Heads in SaaS startups



## Lead Fields to Collect

1. **Full Name** – Used for personalization in outreach.
2. **Job Title** – Identifies decision-makers.
3. **Company Name** – Helps in account-based targeting.
4. **Industry** – Ensures relevance to SaaS domain.
5. **Location** – Filters leads by target regions.
6. **Work Email** – Primary contact method for outreach.



## Workflow Stages

### 1\. Search Setup (Start)

* Use Apollo People Search with filters:

  * Industry: SaaS
  * Job Titles: Founder, CEO, Marketing Head
  * Location: US, UK, India
  * Company Size: 10–500 employees



### 2\. Lead Discovery

* Apollo generates a list of matching profiles
* Preview lead details within Apollo dashboard



### 3\. Data Collection

* Save selected leads to a list in Apollo
* Export data (CSV or CRM integration) including all chosen fields



### 4\. Qualification Filtering

Apply conditions:

* **Job Role:** Must contain Founder, CEO, or Marketing
* **Company Size:** Between 10–500 employees
* **Industry:** Must be SaaS or related tech

Only leads satisfying all criteria are retained



### 5\. Final Action (Review \& Handoff)

* Remove duplicates or incomplete records
* Segment leads (Hot / Warm based on seniority)
* Export to CRM or Google Sheets
* Assign to sales team for outreach campaigns



## Outcome

* Focused, high-quality lead list
* Faster prospecting process
* Improved conversion potential for outreach campaigns
